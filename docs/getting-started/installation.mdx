# Account Setup

Get started with StarHash in minutes.

## Create an Account

1. Visit [StarHash Studio](https://studio.starhash.dev)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Choose your sign-up method:
   - **Google** - One-click signup with your Google account
   - **GitHub** - Sign in with your GitHub account
   - **Email** - Register with email and password

## Verify Your Email

If you signed up with email:

1. Check your inbox for a verification email
2. Click the verification link
3. You'll be redirected to the Studio

<Info>
  Didn't receive the email? Check your spam folder or click "Resend" on the login page.
</Info>

## Complete Your Profile

After signing in for the first time:

1. Enter your organization name
2. Select your primary country
3. Choose your preferred USSD provider (optional)
4. Click **Complete Setup**

## Workspace Overview

Once logged in, you'll see your workspace:

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of your flows and activity |
| **Flows** | Create and manage USSD flows |
| **Instances** | View deployed flows |
| **Sessions** | Debug user sessions |
| **Analytics** | Usage statistics |
| **Settings** | Account and team settings |
